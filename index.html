FOUR LAYOUT RULES

Emptiness

A simple rule that is often violated: put elements with a similar meaning next to each other and surround them with empty space. Emptiness is a powerful means of influence.

Grid

The dimensions of the elements and the distances between them fit into a harmonious scheme - it is called a grid. If the item falls out, the viewer can notice it.

Fonts

No more than three fonts per site. One for headings, one for all other texts, and one for highlighting important words. Should be enough.

Colors

There are usually two colors on the site: lead and accent. You can choose a good color pair due to observation or special tools.
